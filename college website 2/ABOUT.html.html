ABOUT US

A Decade TO Cherish

Kakinada Institute of Engineering and Technology (KIET) endeavors to groom expertly skilled, socially and environmentally conscious, industry-ready, global citizens and through that navigate towards the socio-technoeconomic transformation of the region and beyond. The college has seen tremendous growth since its Inception in 2001 by Mr. P.V.Viswam, Founder & Chairman. The college is currently affiliated with Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Kakinada and approved by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE).
